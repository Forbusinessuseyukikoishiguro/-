CSS Properties to Fix Line Break Issues:

  • word-wrap: break-word; - Allows long words to be broken and wrap onto the next line
  • overflow-wrap: break-word; - Similar to word-wrap, newer standard name
  • word-break: normal; - Controls word breaking rules
  • hyphens: auto; - Adds hyphens when breaking words across lines
